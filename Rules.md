# RULES

  **Redcraft Community Rules**

These rules apply to both the **Discord server** and the **Minecraft server**.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Chat Rules**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**1. Respect Everyone**
• Treat all members with respect.
• No harassment, bullying, racism, or discrimination.

**2. Keep Chat Clean**
• Swearing, offensive language, or inappropriate talk is not allowed.

**3. No Mass Mentioning**
• Do not spam-ping members, staff, or roles.
• Unnecessary @everyone/@here is forbidden.

**4. No Spam or Flooding**
• Don’t spam messages, emojis, or repeated text.

**5. No Advertising**
• No self-promo, server invites, or unrelated links without permission.

**6. No Toxic Behavior**
• Starting drama, baiting arguments, or causing problems will not be tolerated.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Minecraft Rules / Survival**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**7. PvP is Allowed**
• PvP is enabled in certain areas.
• If you enter those zones, you accept the risk.

**8. Raiding / Stealing / Griefing**
• Allowed ONLY if the base has NO protection barrier or claim.
• Protected bases cannot be raided or griefed.

**9. Protect Your Base**
• If your base isn’t protected, it can be raided.

**10. No Hacks or Cheats**
• No x-ray, fly hacks, dupes, exploits, auto-clickers, or unfair modifications.

**11. No Spawn Killing**
• Repeatedly killing or trapping players at spawn is not allowed.

**12. No Lag Machines**
• Builds made to lag or crash the server are forbidden.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Bedwars Rules**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**13. No Teaming in Solos**
• Teaming in solo matches is not allowed.

**14. No Cross-Teaming**
• Helping enemy teams is forbidden.

**15. No Exploits**
• Bug abuse, glitches, or map exploits are not allowed.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Skyblock Rules**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**16. No Scamming**
• All trades must be fair.

**17. No Island Griefing**
• Do not destroy islands unless permitted.

**18. No Economy Abuse**
• Exploiting bugs or abusing systems is forbidden.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Punishments**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Punishments may include:
• Warning
• Mute
• Kick
• Temporary Ban
• Permanent Ban

Staff decisions are final.

Failure to follow these rules may result in punishment.
Enjoy your time in **Redcraft**.


# COURT RULES

**Redcraft Court Rules**

These rules apply to all court cases inside Redcraft.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Case Rules**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**1. Server-Only Cases**
• You may only sue for events, actions, or problems that happened inside the Discord server, group chat, or Minecraft server.
• Real-life issues or outside-server drama cannot be used in court.

**2. No Suing During an Active Case**
• Anything said or done inside the courtroom while a case is ongoing cannot be used as a new lawsuit.
• Courtroom actions stay inside that case only.

**3. Trespassing / Base Entry**
• If you leave a team, faction, or group and are told you are no longer allowed at their base, returning without permission may result in a lawsuit.
• If the base owner has proof, they may win the case.

**4. No Creating Random Cases Mid-Trial**
• You cannot create or bring up another case during an active trial without permission.

**5. Judge Permission Required**
• If you want to open or mention another case while one is active, you must get permission from:
• The Judge
**OR**
• The person suing you.

**6. Daily Court Limit**
• A maximum of 3 court cases may be held each day.
• Once the limit is reached, all new cases must wait until the next day.

**7. Priority Cases**
• Judges or staff may prioritize more serious cases if needed.

**8. No Bypassing the Daily Limit**
• Spamming, forcing, or attempting to create extra cases after the limit is reached is forbidden.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Language & Representation Rules**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**9. Proper English Required**
• Lawyers, the person suing, and the person defending must use understandable English and proper spelling during court.

**10. Failure to Communicate Properly**
• If a person cannot properly explain their case or defense in English, the opposing side may automatically receive what they are suing for, unless the Judge decides otherwise.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Evidence Rules**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**11. Proof is Required**
• Screenshots, recordings, logs, or witnesses may be used as evidence.

**12. Fake Evidence**
• Editing, faking, or lying about evidence will result in punishment.

**13. Judge Must Review Evidence**
• The Judge must review all evidence fairly before making a final decision.

**14. Judge Responsibility**
• If a Judge ignores evidence, abuses power, or makes unfair rulings, they may be removed from their position.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Courtroom Behavior**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**15. Attendance**
• Everyone involved in the case is expected to attend court when it begins.

**16. Leaving Court**
• If someone leaves during court, they cannot automatically be punished, as crashes, disconnects, or real-life reasons may happen.

**17. Respect the Court**
• Do not interrupt the Judge or the person speaking.

**18. No Spamming or Disruption**
• Mic spam, chat flooding, or causing disruptions during court is not allowed.

**19. Stay on Topic**
• Keep discussion related to the active case only.

**20. Judge’s Decision is Final**
• Once a ruling is made, it is final unless new valid proof is presented for appeal.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Jail Sentencing Rules**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**21. Jail Sentences**
• If a player is sentenced to jail by the Judge, they must remain in jail for the full amount of time decided by the court.

**22. Serving Time**
• The sentence timer begins once the player is placed in jail.

**23. Leaving During Sentence**
• If a player leaves jail before their sentence is complete without a valid reason, they may be fined an amount decided by the Judge.

**24. Valid Reasons**
• Crashing, disconnecting, emergencies, or other reasonable excuses may be accepted by the Judge.

**25. Avoiding Jail**
• Attempting to escape, exploit, or avoid jail punishment may result in extra jail time, larger fines, or further punishment.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 **Punishments**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Breaking court rules may result in:
• Case dismissal
• Fines
• Temporary mute
• Loss of the case
• Jail time
• Temporary ban
• Permanent ban (for severe abuse)

Follow the rules and keep court fair for everyone.
